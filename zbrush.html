ZBrush is a digital sculpting and painting software developed by Pixologic. It is popular among artists and professionals in the film, television, and games industries for its ability to create high-quality 3D models and textures with a natural and intuitive workflow.

Features:

  • Sculpting tools for creating detailed 3D models
  • Texturing tools for adding realistic materials and surfaces to models
  • Painting tools for adding color and details to models
  • 3D brush system for sculpting and shaping models
  • Animation tools for creating 3D animations

ZBrush was originally released in 1999 and has since become one of the most widely used tools in the 3D graphics industry for creating detailed 3D models and high-resolution 2D images. It is used by artists in the film, television, video game, and other industries for creating characters, creatures, environments, and special effects.

ZBrush is a powerful tool that allows users to sculpt, paint, and texture 3D models using a variety of brushes and tools. It features a highly intuitive and user-friendly interface that makes it easy to work with even for artists with little or no 3D modeling experience.

One of the key features of ZBrush is its ability to work with high-resolution models and images. It also has a wide range of features and tools for working with displacement maps, normal maps, and 3D texturing.

Pricing: ZBrush is available for purchase, with prices starting at $895. You can learn more and purchase a license on the Pixologic website.
